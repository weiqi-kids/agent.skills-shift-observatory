BetterBrain | Software Engineer | Remote (USA)

About BetterBrain
BetterBrain (https://www.betterbrain.com) is at the forefront of dementia prevention. We offer the most comprehensive platform to help our members prevent neurodegenerative diseases and optimize their cognition. Role Overview We're rapidly growing and are seeking a talented Software Engineer to join our team and help shape the future of preventative neurology. As a full-stack engineer with experience in high-growth startups and building AI products, you’ll play a key role in our development.

Key Responsibilities
Develop and maintain web applications using our tech stack: NextJS, React, Typescript, and Supabase. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and implement new features.

Requirements
4-5+ years of full-stack development experience. Proven experience in high-growth startups. Strong background in building AI products. Proficiency in NextJS, React, Typescript, and Python. Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

How to Apply
Email us at adi@betterbrain.com with the subject line "HN Hiring". Please include: - A copy of your resume. - A brief description of why you think you’re a good fit for this role (for example - a project you owned)
