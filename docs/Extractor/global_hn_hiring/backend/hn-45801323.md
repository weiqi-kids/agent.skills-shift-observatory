Tandem Health | Senior/Staff Software Engineers | On-site in Stockholm, Sweden | Full time At Tandem Health we're building a clinician copilot to allow clinicians to focus on care rather than administration. Our core product listens in to the patient-clinician encounter to generate medical records, allowing clinicians to save 5-10 minutes per patient.
