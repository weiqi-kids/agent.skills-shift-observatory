The Alan Turing Institute | Open Source AI Fellowship | Hybrid (UK) | Full-time (min 0.8 FTE) | Secondment

The Open Source AI Fellowship 2025 is currently open for applications: https://www.turing.ac.uk/work-turing/open-source-ai-fellowsh...

A $1 million programme funded by Meta's grant to The Alan Turing Institute that will bring exceptional AI engineers into UK government for 12 months.

Fellows with expertise in specialised AI model development and secure systems engineering will be seconded within the Department for Science, Innovation and Technology and work alongside the Incubator for Artificial Intelligence to develop high-impact AI use-cases that build on open source AI models to transform public services.

Fellowships begin in January 2026, support hybrid working, and reimburse salary costs up to £85,000 per annum.

Applicants must be employed by a UK organisation to enable the secondment, have the right to work in the UK and min 5 years residency.

Applications close Monday 18 August 2025 at 12:00 (midday) BST.
