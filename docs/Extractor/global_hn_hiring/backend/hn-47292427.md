CiceroAI | Founding Forward Deployed Engineer | Houston, TX | Remote or Onsite | Full Time

Cicero transformed the practice of law in ancient Rome. We are bringing that spirit back.

At CiceroAI (ciceroailaw.com), we are building the operating system for the front office of law firms — automating intake, qualification, and client onboarding for the 165,000+ law firms across the United States. Our goal is simple: help law firms respond instantly, sign better cases faster, and deliver justice to millions of people who currently wait days or weeks just to be heard.

We are looking for a Founding Forward Deployed Engineer to work directly with us and our clients and bring Cicero to life inside real law firms.

This role is for an exceptional engineer who: enjoys working directly with customers, can rapidly ship and iterate in production, wants to build technology that operates inside real businesses from day one.

You will deploy Cicero at law firms, build product alongside users, and help shape the foundation of the company.

If you want to build quickly, work closely with customers, and help transform how legal services operate, we would love to talk.
