## 職位描述

We’re looking for ahands-on Digital Marketing Manager (B2B)who enjoys turning a strong product story into measurable growth. At adstrong, you’re not just running campaigns, you’re shaping how agencies and e-commerce brands perceive and experience one of the leading Google CSS players worldwide.

## Tasks

Performance Marketing Ownership.

Plan, run, and optimize performance marketing campaigns across Google Ads, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, and Meta Ads.

Budget & KPI Management.

Own paid media budgets end to end, including pacing, scaling, and performance-based allocation against clear KPIs.

Messaging & Conversion.

Translate product strengths into clear, conversion-focused messaging across ads, funnels, and landing pages.

Funnel & Landing Page Optimization.

Test and scale funnels, creatives, and landing pages based on performance data and user behavior.

Tracking & Attribution.

Set up and maintain tracking and attribution accross Google Tag Manager and CRM-based tools like HubSpot.

Analysis & Optimization.

Analyze performance across channels and the full funnel, turning insights into actionable improvements.

B2B Demand Generation.

Use LinkedIn as a core B2B demand channel and support webinars and events where relevant.

Cross-Functional Collaboration.

Work closely with the team, taking ownership of outcomes rather than activities.

## Requirements

Performance Marketing Experience.

3-5 years of experience in digital marketing, with strong hands-on expertise in performance marketing within a B2B or SaaS context.

Paid Media Expertise.

Proven experience managing CPC-, CPM-, and CPA-based campaigns, primarily on Google Ads and/or LinkedIn Campaign Manager. Meta Ads are a nice-to-have or a strong alternative if Google Ads or LinkedIn experience is lighter.

Tracking & Attribution.

Hands-on experience with Google Tag Manager, web analytics tools (GA4, Plausible, or similar), and CRM-based attribution systems such as HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, or ActiveCampaign.

Analytical & Data-Driven.

Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret data and translate insights into optimization actions that drive pipeline and revenue.

Outcome-Oriented Mindset.

You focus on impact over activity and optimize toward clear business goals like efficiency, pipeline, and growth.

Hands-on & Proactive.

You take ownership, move fast, and learn through testing rather than waiting for perfect conditions.

Collaborative & Low Ego.

You value open discussion, shared learning, and improving results together.

Language Skills.

Business-fluent English (written and spoken). German is a plus.

Nice to Have.

Experience withWordPress and Webflow or Framer, includinglanding page optimization; basicdesign collaborationusing tools likeFigma or Canva; support ofB2B webinars and onsite events; experience working in astartup or scale-up environment; and familiarity withAI toolsfor marketing analysis or creative workflows.
