## 職位描述

Nexun, wspierany przez Marguerite – wiodący paneuropejski fundusz infrastrukturalny – to platforma zajmująca się rozwojem projektów odnawialnych źródeł energii, aktywna w Hiszpanii, Francji, Niemczech, Włoszech, Polsce i Austrii. (Nexun, supported by Marguerite, a pan-European infrastructure fund, is a platform for renewable energy project development active in Spain, France, Germany, Italy, Poland and Austria.)

**Role**
As Land Manager in the renewable energy sector, you will be responsible for identifying, acquiring and managing land for renewable energy projects (photovoltaic farms and energy storage).

**Tasks**
- Search for suitable land for renewable energy investments (PV farms, energy storage)
- Conduct analysis and assessment of land potential for renewable energy projects
- Negotiate lease and purchase terms with landowners
- Prepare documentation for permits and environmental decisions
- Collaborate with the project team and legal department
- Monitor the real estate market and analyse potential areas
- Work with local authorities and other stakeholders

**Requirements**
- Experience in land acquisition in the renewable energy sector
- Knowledge of legal regulations related to renewable energy and real estate management
- Negotiation skills and ability to build relationships with landowners, municipalities and stakeholders
- Analytical capabilities and knowledge of land assessment tools
