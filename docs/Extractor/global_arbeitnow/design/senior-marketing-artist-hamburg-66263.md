## Your mission

Join us as a(Senior) Marketing Artistat the intersection of creativity and performance. You’ll lead the creation of mobile-first assets, shape and test new creative concepts, and optimize creatives together with UA teams to drive growth at scale. If this sounds like you, we’d love to see your application!

## In this role you will:

- Design and produce high-quality, mobile-optimized visual assets, including app icons, screenshots, promotional banners, videos, and other marketing materials.

- Organize in-house shootings to create compelling marketing content.

- Collaborate closely with User Acquisition Managers to create new concepts to find new ways to market our brands.

- Conduct market research and stay up-to-date with trends to identify best practices and inspire creative ideas.

- Work effectively with others, contributing innovative and creative ideas to the team.

- Analyze the performance of marketing assets and iterate based on data-driven insights to continuously optimize creative output.

Beyond your operational responsibilities, you will also embody and champion our core values every day:

- Elevate Together– Collaboration is our strength. We support each other, embrace diversity, and grow as a team.

- Xray Transparency– Open communication builds trust and strong relationships. We value honesty, inclusion, and accountability.

- Master Feedback– We challenge and support each other through honest, caring feedback.

- Own the Outcome– We take ownership, move fast, and turn ideas into results.

- Xcute Smart– Keep it simple, act pragmatically, and focus on what truly matters.

## Your profile

- 4–5 years of relevant experience in graphic design, digital art, or a related field, specifically within mobile marketing or the mobile gaming industry.

- Strong portfolio showcasing your projects and demonstrating your experience in the field.

- Technical experience in composing marketing creatives in line with briefings.

- Solid understanding of the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, AfterEffects) and experience with motion graphics and video editing.

- Capability to drive conclusions out of creative performance and take learnings based on performance data for future creative productions.

- Experience managing the entire process of publishing gameplay videos, from recording to editing and exporting.

- Experience in creating playable ads is a plus.

- Strong Communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a cross-functional team.

## Nice to have:

- Ability to create 3D animated scenes, take renders, add animations or effects, and export videos for publishing with After Effects.
