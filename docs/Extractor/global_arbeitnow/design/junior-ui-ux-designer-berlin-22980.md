## 職位描述

Immersive VR Experiences for Live Entertainment

L1VE GmbH is a fast-growing media and technology company rethinking how people experience sports, music and live entertainment. With cutting-edge 12K, 3D and 360° streaming technology and a global portfolio of premium rights, we bring live events to leading VR platforms in a truly immersive way.

Our goal is simple: create experiences that don’t feel like technology, but like being there. To get there, we design intuitive, high-quality interfaces that make immersive experiences easy and enjoyable to use.

We’re looking for aJunior UI/UX Designer (f/m/x)who’s excited to learn, grow and help shape the user experience of our products together with Design, Product and Engineering.

## Tasks

- Design UI screens, user flows and interface states based on existing concepts and guidelines

- Help maintain and evolve design systems, components and layout rules

- Create cross-platform interfaces for web, mobile and, in the future, VR

- Build clickable prototypes to quickly validate UX and UI ideas

- Work closely with the Design Lead to iterate fast and ensure quality

- Align designs with Product and Engineering and consider technical constraints

- Prepare clean handovers and documentation for development

## Requirements

- First experience in UI/UX design through a junior role, internship or relevant projects

- Confident working with Figma, including components, auto layout, variants and states

- Good understanding of UX basics, user flows and interaction patterns

- Initial experience with design systems and reusable UI components

- Basic understanding of web and app development concepts

- Curious, reliable and comfortable working in a fast-moving product environment

Language Requirements

- German and English, fluent in written and spoken communication

## Benefits

- Learn and grow while working on real products with global reach

- Get hands-on experience with VR, live streaming and immersive tech

- Work closely with experienced designers, product managers and engineers

- Enjoy a culture with short decision paths, open feedback and room for ideas

- Be part of a team shaping the future of immersive live entertainment

Join L1VE and help create immersive experiences that captivate audiences worldwide.
