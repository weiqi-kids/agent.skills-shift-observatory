## Your mission

We’re looking for a (Senior) User Acquisition / Performance Marketing Manager to join exmox and help shape our growth. Bring your creativity and expertise to make a real impact on user acquisition and business results, while working in a collaborative, data-driven environment.

In this role you will:

- Plan, execute, and optimize user acquisition campaigns across performance platforms, including Google Ads, Facebook, IronSource, Unity, Liftoff, and others.

- Analyze data and optimize campaign performance towards Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

- Monitor key metrics, adjust strategies, and make data-driven decisions to improve results.

- Drive A/B testing in order to improve UA performance

- Track and report key metrics regularly.

- Build and maintain strong relationships with advertising partners.

- Collaborate cross-functionally to develop and implement innovative user acquisition strategies.

- You proactively stay up-to-date on industry trends, best practices.

Beyond your operational responsibilities, you will also embody and champion our core values every day:

- Elevate Together– Collaboration is our strength. We support each other, embrace diversity, and grow as a team.

- Xray Transparency– Open communication builds trust and strong relationships. We value honesty, inclusion, and accountability.

- Master Feedback– We challenge and support each other through honest, caring feedback.

- Own the Outcome– We take ownership, move fast, and turn ideas into results.

- Xcute Smart– Keep it simple, act pragmatically, and focus on what truly matters.

## Your profile

- Have a university degree in Marketing, Analytics, Business or a similar qualification.

- Bring 5+ years of relevant experience in performance marketing, and experience in the mobile gaming industry is a plus.

- You have experience in managing Google Ads, Facebook Ads, TikTok, SDK video networks (Applovin, Unity, Ironsource) in US and main Tier1 markets.

- You are creative-minded: you don’t need to design, but you “get” the creative side of marketing.

- Strong analytical skills and a strategic, data-driven mindset.

- Eager to learn, grow, and thrive in a dynamic environment.

- You are dedicated, adaptable, and an excellent communicator with colleagues and stakeholders.

- Fluent in English; knowledge of German is very welcome (other languages are a plus).

- You have a high affinity for mobile apps and a genuine enthusiasm for games.

If you’re nodding along while reading this, that’s a good sign. Apply with your CV in English we’re excited to meet you!
