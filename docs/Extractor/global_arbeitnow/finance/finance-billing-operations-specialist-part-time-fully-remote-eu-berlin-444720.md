## 職位描述

We’re looking for someone who enjoys bringing order to contracts, invoices, and payments. This is a fully remote role with a company headquartered in Munich, Germany. Business-level English is essential, and a good understanding of German is required.

## Tasks

- Manage and maintain customer, contract, and billing data across systems.

- Oversee approximately 250 outgoing invoices per month (e.g. via Easybill).

- Handle contracts, contract changes, and cancellations.

- Monitor incoming payments via bank and payment providers (e.g. Stripe) and assign them correctly.

- Spot missing or delayed payments and follow up proactively.

- Handle payment follow-ups in a professional, friendly, and solution-oriented way, with a focus on maintaining strong customer relationships.

- Collaborate closely with external accounting and tax advisors.

- Support the implementation and optimization of finance, billing, and CRM tools.

- Maintain a clear, reliable contract and billing database.

## Requirements

- Experience.At least2–4 years of hands-on experiencein finance operations, billing, preferably in a B2B setup.

- Process & Precision.Highly structured, detail-oriented, and accountable for data accuracy, invoicing, and on-time payments.

- Tools & Systems.High confidence working with CRM, billing, and payment tools is a must. Experience withHubSpot, Easybill, Agicap, Stripe, or similar systemsis strongly preferred.

- Contracts & Payments.Solid understanding ofcommercial contracts, invoicing logic, and payment flows, including recurring billing and service periods.

- Customer Communication.Professional, friendly, and solution-oriented communication with customers on payment topics.

- Tax & Compliance.Familiarity withGerman and EU tax requirements.

- Languages.Business-fluentEnglish and German(C1 level).

- Education.A bachelor’s degree in finance, accounting, business administration or similar.

- Remote Readiness.Comfortable working independently with a high level of ownership.
