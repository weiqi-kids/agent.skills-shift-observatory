We’re looking for a Senior Web3 Frontend Engineer (Lead) who enjoys building great interfaces, leading others, and helping customers succeed.

What you’ll do

- Lead frontend architecture for complex Web3 applications (dashboards, protocol UIs, admin panels)

- 3+ years of frontend engineering experience

- Strong expertise in React / Next.js / TypeScript

- Hands-on Web3 experience (wallets, signing flows, contract interactions)

- Solid UX intuition for financial and DeFi products

- Experience leading frontend work, mentoring engineers, and owning delivery

- Familiarity with data-heavy UIs (indexers, subgraphs, real-time data)

- A builder mindset with a genuine desire to help others succeed

- Experience with Web3 libraries (ethers, viem)
