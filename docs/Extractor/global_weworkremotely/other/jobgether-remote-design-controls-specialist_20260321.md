## 職缺描述

This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. The role is a Design Controls Specialist focused on ensuring compliance and efficiency in design control processes. The specialist will collaborate with Regulatory, Quality, and R&D departments to drive improvements across product development processes, ensuring adherence to global regulatory standards while supporting timely product releases.

**Key responsibilities include:**
- Partner in optimizing and streamlining design control requirements for medical devices
- Lead process development and improvement projects with cross-functional teams globally
- Conduct and develop training on design controls and related process changes
- Ensure conformity of processes and records to global regulatory standards
- Audit design records to ensure compliance and address non-conformances
- Perform gap analyses against international regulatory standards and propose updates
